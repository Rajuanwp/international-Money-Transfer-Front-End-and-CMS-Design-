About Us

Who We Are

Danesh Exchange is a world class money exchanger serving people Australia wide for more than one decade. Whether you need to need on-spot money exchanging services for your foreign tours, Danesh Exchange is the place to be since 2009.

Danesh Exchange also provides live updated exchange rates for any currency that you need. Our live exchange rates eliminate the chance of you getting lower rates than you deserve. Our well trained staff members offer quick and friendly service where you don’t even have to pay any additional commission on your transactions. Highest cash exchange rates along with quick services are our forte and we aim to bring in many updated versions of money exchanging keeping up to date with the modern world that we are in.

Danesh Exchange also provides the option for on-line currency booking where you can book your required currency and pick it up on your convenience eliminating the chance of a wasted visit. We care for you precious time and effort and have in our stock over 65 international currencies that are always available on spot as well.
